Mitigation of disease pandemics with DeFi

Tue Aug 8 1995 · 3 minute read · (last modified on August 8, 1995)

When Bill Gates was asked in a Vox interview[^watch-the-video] on what he feared most, his reply was not climate change or nuclear war.

Mr. Gates was most worried about pandemics, deadly diseases that spread quickly over large regions, he goes ahead to cite some of the biggest “death charts” of history.

On talking more on these “death charts”, there where several spikes in which a large number of people died in a very short period.

The first he mentions is World War I, the second one is World War II, the third spike was as big as World War I (30 million deaths)
but it was not a war it was the spanish flu.

Pandemics are real & unpredictable as we have seen with Ebola, SARs, and at the time of this writting, the Corona virus.

The problem is that, we are often not prepared for pandemics. For instance during the Ebola pandemic in West Africa, 11,000 died simply because the reponsible beaureacrats could not mobilse enough resources in time.

There was an idea from the World Health Organinsation (WHO) to use create a kind of insurance market they call a Pandemic bond.

How it would work is that, a pool of money would be put up by speculators. The WHO would then countinously pay the speculators a kind of insurance premium. But if a new Ebola out break occured in a new country, the WHO would use the pool of money (put up by the speculators) to facilitate the mitigation of Ebola in that region. This is obviously an over simplification of the 200 page document explaing how these pandemics bonds actually work but I hope you get the idea? Its basically health insurance for everyone subsidised by the WHO.

The pandemic bonds have however become controvertial probably due to their poor mechanism design. Despite this fact, I think mechanism designers can come up with better mechanisms resembling these “pandemic bonds” that actually work in a society.

We can have several DeFi (Decentralized Finance) powered applications to mitigate several problems around disease.

For instance; - Animal husbandry bussineses can pay these premiums to mitigate risks around animal disease pandemics like birdflu or mad cow disease. Charities or governments can pay these premiums to to secure resources for fighting pandemic out breaks like the recent Corona virus. The recent Corona virus for example is so new, there exists no cure. In such a case, the pool speculators put up would be used for research towards the creation of a vaccinne.

Another way of using DeFi to mitigate disease pandemics is by leveraging prediction markets like Augur & Gnosis to create an “event derivative” that may assist in the mitigation of a disease. Event derivatives are markets that pay people for accurately predicting events in the future.

An example is a market we can call, “Will it rain on Thursday, next week?”. Before Thusday next week, people can bet “YES” or “NO” on if it will really rain on that day. If it rains, people who bet “YES” win the bet & people who voted “NO” have their money transfered to people that bet “YES”.

Coming back to disease pandemics, we can have a question like “Will there be a cure for Corona viral infections?”. In theory, this “event derivative” should incetivise the release of information that may lead to the creation of an Corona viral infection.

The market can also be subsidized by an entity by increasing the stakes to be won if incase the market outcome ins “YES”.

This seems like something we can do today, why don’t we do it then?
